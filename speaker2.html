Hello!

Imoh Engwoh

I am the Technical lead - GIS and Remote Sensing for TerrAfrik Consults, a Pan-African space intelligence firm, leveraging the power of cutting edge Geographic Information Technology to drive sustainable development and support business decisions.

With over eight (8) years of cognate experience in the application of location intelligence to several business sectors and SDGs, he is adept at unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV) and satellite synergies for mapping and remote sensing applications.

Imoh holds a Master's degree in Geo-information technology from the African Regional Institute for Geospatial Science and Technology (Under the auspices of United Nations Economic Commission for Africa - UNECA), Ile-Ife, Osun State, Nigeria. Also, he possesses a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Management from the University of Calabar, Cross River State of Nigeria. His drive for professional development has earned him several certifications from the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) and UNICEF-powered African Drone and Data Academy, Malawi.
