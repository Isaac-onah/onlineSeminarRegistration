Hello!

Jones Bassey

I am a founding member of JOSEG Robotics, and currently her chief executive officer(C.E.O).

JOSEG Robotics is a startup that was founded in 2017, and was incorporated with the Nigerian corporate Affairs commission(CAC) on the 5th of February, 2019. JOSEG started as a project group, developing and building drones for different purposes and applications which mostly was academic. And later diversified into drone applications, like aerial mapping, training, repairs and consultancy.

Mr. Bassey holds a Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Electronics Engineering from the university of uyo, Akwa Ibom state, Nigeria. As well as a remote sensing/ Geographic information system(GIS) certification from the National Space Research and Development Agency, an affiliate of the Federal Ministry of Science and Technology, Nigeria. He is a hardware Engineer, majoring on both ground and aerial robots. A drone tech expert cum enthusiast.
